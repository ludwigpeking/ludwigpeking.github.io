A simulation: Rectangular Parcels & Voronoi Pattern

Independent Study, 2022, (javascript)

Link To p5*js Editor

1) Parcelization: Voronoi Claiming, Rectangular Parcels from Voronoi Claiming of Aligned Nuclei on Straight Street:

It might be obvious that the parcelization in the city has an origin from the informal occupation made by individuals in a Voronoi diagram-like pattern. This study discovers and proves that rectangular parcelization can be generated too by the claiming on a Voronoi pattern, where nuclei are aligned on the street.

2) Street Pattern: Pathfinding on borders:

This study demonstrates that street patterns can be a result of the left-over of claiming on the property edges due to efficiency driven spontaneous choice.
