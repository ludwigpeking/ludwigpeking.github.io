Self-organization of settlement based on Value Grid system

This study demonstrates value grid based procedural town map generation. Each individual, based on their own profession and interest, weights the value grid differently, therefore will have a different choice of location for settlement. For example, a warrior/lord eyes on a location with defensible terrain; a farmer picks where land is abundant and mutual protection is provided by neighborhood; a merchant looks at the amount of foot traffic. Professions/Castes and their interest value

  • lord/warrior/protector: defensible terrain, secured fresh water access, ...
  • farmer/tenant: more available arable land (per capita) in manageable distance, protector in reachable distance, natural surveillance provided by the neighborhood
  • Trader: enough foot traffic pass by to sustain business, protector in reachable distance, natural surveillance provided by the neighborhood, â€¦
