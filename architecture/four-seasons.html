The City of Four Seasons, Shenyang, 2020-2021
Residential, Retail, Redevelopment,
As Director of Design, development team with Vanke Co.
Collaborators: RUFArchitects, Zheng Yi Interior Architects ; LOMA landscape architects

The The City of Four Seasons Project(四季都会) is a transformation of an unfinished development located on the outskirts of Shenyang city. The public amenities of the project are highlighted by a greenhouse brimming with the vibrancy of spring, designed to uplift and soothe the spirits of residents during the lengthy winter months. The original unfinished development was envisioned in the so-called "Tuscan style." To honor this, the renovation incorporates archways and faux-brick finishes to echo the initial design. However, a modern touch is infused, characterized by cleaner, more streamlined, and refined architectural details to breathe new life and contemporary elegance into the space.
